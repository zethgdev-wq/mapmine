Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the data?
Our data is scraped directly from Google Maps in real-time, so it reflects what's currently live. Phone numbers and addresses come straight from business listings. Emails are verified before delivery. We consistently achieve 99%+ accuracy on phone numbers and addresses, and 95%+ on email deliverability.
What format do I get the data in?
All plans include CSV files that work with Excel, Google Sheets, and any CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Instantly, Smartlead, etc.). Pro and Enterprise plans also include formatted Excel files with filtered columns ready for immediate use.
Can you scrape businesses outside the US?
Yes. We can scrape any location covered by Google Maps, including Canada, UK, Australia, and most other countries. Just specify the city and country in your order. International orders use the same pricing and turnaround times.
How fast is delivery?
Starter orders are delivered within 24 hours. Pro orders ship same-day (typically within 4-6 hours). Enterprise orders with custom requirements are delivered within 24-48 hours depending on volume. You'll receive your file via email.
What's your refund policy?
If the data doesn't meet the accuracy standards we promise, we'll re-scrape at no cost or issue a full refund. We stand behind every order. If you're not satisfied, email us within 7 days of delivery and we'll make it right.
